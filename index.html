About This Issue

Hello, this is George, and thanks for reading this Summer, 2025 issue of Impress.

In this issue, we play with words with puzzles and puns from Nick and myself. We learn about slang with some examples from the Yotsuba Gakuen English Club. And we learn about haniwa with Alice and the staff from the Akabori Museum of History. There's a cool rooster you can check out there.

Our hot summer is almost over and autumn is coming, so we hope you enjoy the last of the summer heat with this issue. See you back in school soon!
